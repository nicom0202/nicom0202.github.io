Shasta Climb - June 2023

I climbed and summited Mount Shasta (14,180 ft) with my brother (Jake), my uncle (Rudy), and my friend Hannah in June 2023. This was one of hardest things I've ever done and I'll gladly do it again. Although the pictures are beautiful, they do not truly encompass the sheer size of the mountain. This was one of the most memorable adventures I've had and I couldn't have done it without the truly wonderful company joining me.


Baking

I started my baking journey at the beginning of 2023 and I continue to improve my craft after every bake. Baking has become a weekly Sunday tradition to help me destress from the week and get me in the right mindset for the next. It has become my therapeutic activity. I hope to someday open a bakery of my own.


Gypsy 2018-2022

This was my dog Gypsy. She was the sweetest, goofiest and most interesting dog I've ever had. She was truly one of a kind. Rest in peace.


Guatemala 2022

My family is from Guatemala and I thought it was time to visit the motherland. My girlfirend (Val) joined me on this adventure. We stayed in El Paredon, Antigua, and Lake Atitlan. We met some of the kindest, warmest people I had ever met.


Greece 2021

I visited Greece in the Summer of 2021. My girlfriend (Val) and I stayed in Crete and Athens. In Crete, the food was truly amazing and I got to visit the Acropolis in Athens. Discovered my favorite food is squid.


Spain & Portugal 2022

I went to Barcelona and Lisbon in the Winter of 2022. I got to see the Sagrada Familia and Christmas markets in Barcelona. Lisbon had some of the best Pastel de Nata I've had. I ate an octupus every day.


France & London 2020

When I went to Paris in the Winter of 2020, it was my first time leaving the country. Biting into my first pastry was heavenly, and was one of the first influences for me to start my baking journey. This trip opened my eyes to travelling the world.
