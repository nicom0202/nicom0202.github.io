Shasta Climb - June 2023

I climbed and summited Mount Shasta (14,180 ft) with my brother (Jake), my uncle (Rudy), and my friend Hannah in June 2023. This was one of hardest things I've ever done and I'll gladly do it again. Although the pictures are beautiful, they do not truly encompass the sheer size of the mountain. One of the most memorable adventures I've had and I couldn't have done it without the truly wonderful company joining me.
